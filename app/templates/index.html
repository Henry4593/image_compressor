Our Story

Compressio was born out of frustration. As a web developer, I often struggled with large image files that slowed down my projects. After countless hours searching for reliable tools, I decided to create my own.

This project has improved my workflow and has become a valuable resource for others. This is a Portfolio Project for Alx Africa.
